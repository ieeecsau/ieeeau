About

The IEEE Computer Society is one of the professional societies under the umbrella of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). It is dedicated to advancing the theory, practice, and application of computer and information processing technology.

Our Mission

The IEEE Computer Society's mission is generally focused on advancing the theory, practice, and application of computer and information processing science and technology. It aims to promote the exchange of information, foster professional development, and provide a platform for collaboration and networking among computer professionals worldwide.

Our Vision

The IEEE Computer Society aspires to be a premier organization that facilitates the growth of computer science and technology, fosters innovation, and addresses the evolving needs of professionals and researchers in the computing community.
